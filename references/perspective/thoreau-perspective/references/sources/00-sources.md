# 梭罗 Skill 信息来源 (Sources)

## 一手资料 (Primary Sources)

### 著作
1. **《瓦尔登湖》(Walden, 1854)**
   - Henry David Thoreau. Walden; or, Life in the Woods. 1854.
   - 普林斯顿版：The Writings of Henry D. Thoreau, Princeton University Press

2. **《论公民的不服从》(Civil Disobedience, 1849)**
   - Thoreau, H. D. "Resistance to Civil Government." 1849.
   - 后更名为《论公民的不服从》

3. **《缅因森林》(The Maine Woods, 1864)**
   - Thoreau, H. D. The Maine Woods. Edited by Sophia Thoreau, 1864.

4. **《康科德河与梅里马克河的一周》(A Week on the Concord and Merrimack Rivers, 1849)**

5. **《科德角》(Cape Cod, 1865)**

6. **《散步》(Walking, 1862)**
   - 著名演讲，"荒野是世界的保存地"出处

7. **《梭罗日记》(The Journal of Henry D. Thoreau, 1837-1861)**
   - 约 200 万字，24 年记录
   - 普林斯顿版：The Journal of Henry D. Thoreau, 14 vols., Princeton University Press, 1906.

### 书信
- **《梭罗书信集》(The Correspondence of Henry David Thoreau)**
  - Edited by Walter Harding and Carl Bode, 1958.

## 二手资料 (Secondary Sources)

### 传记
1. **Walter Harding**. *The Days of Henry Thoreau: A Biography*. Knopf, 1965.
   - 权威传记，详细记录生平

2. **Robert D. Richardson Jr.** *Henry Thoreau: A Life of the Mind*. University of California Press, 1986.
   - 思想传记，聚焦哲学发展

3. **Laura Dassow Walls**. *Henry David Thoreau: A Life*. University of Chicago Press, 2017.
   - 最新权威传记

### 文学批评
1. **F.O. Matthiessen**. *American Renaissance: Art and Expression in the Age of Emerson and Whitman*. 1941.
   - 确立梭罗在美国文学经典中的地位

2. **Stanley Cavell**. *The Senses of Walden*. 1972.
   - 哲学解读

3. **Lawrence Buell**. *The Environmental Imagination: Thoreau, Nature Writing, and the Formation of American Culture*. Harvard University Press, 1995.
   - 环保主义视角

### 超验主义研究
1. **Barbara L. Packer**. *The Transcendentalists*. University of Georgia Press, 2007.

2. **Joel Myerson** (ed.). *Transcendentalism: A Reader*. Oxford University Press, 2000.

### 专门研究
1. **Leonard N. Neufeldt**. *The Economist of Thoreau*. 1973.
   - 瓦尔登湖经济分析

2. **Robert E. Grover**. *Thoreau's Visionary Theory of Education*. 1985.

3. **Edmund A. O'Sullivan**. *Thoreau and Social Reform*. 1990.
   - 社会改革思想

### 影响研究
1. **Ronald Grimes**. *Thoreau's Democratic Individualism*. 2010.

2. **Gary Snyder**. *The Practice of the Wild*. 1990.
   - 梭罗对深层生态学的影响

3. **Lawrence Rosenwald**. *The Theory of Moral Sentiment in Thoreau's Political Thought*. 2000.

## 引用原则

1. **优先一手**: 直接引用梭罗原文而非他人解读
2. **学术权威**: 二手资料限于学术出版社和同行评审
3. **跨版本验证**: 关键事实核对多个来源
4. **语境完整**: 引用保持原文语境，不断章取义
